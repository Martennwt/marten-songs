Do I need to know how to code?

No. The Set up Claude course walks you through every step, click by click. If you can install an app on your computer, you can run this.

Do I need to make music or be able to sing?

No. You bring a song, your own or an AI-generated one, together with the lyrics or just an idea. The system turns it into the video, and we point you to the music tools so you are never stuck.

Which niche does it work for?

Any niche. Travel, love and relationships, learning and study, fantasy and storytelling, motivation, history, kids, mindfulness, your brand, your own life. If you can feel it, you can turn it into a song.

Is AI content even allowed on YouTube in 2026?

Yes, when it is transparent and adds value. Faceless slideshow spam gets demonetized. The compliance guide shows you how to disclose AI properly and add the transformation and originality YouTube rewards, so your channel stays monetizable.

What exactly do I get?

One markdown skill file that holds the whole studio, the Set up Claude video course, the niche and angle playbook, the story and meaning framework, the 2026 compliance guide, the branding spec, ready-made examples, and the dashboard template.

The tools are cheap at this scale. A finished song costs from a few cents to about one euro in AI usage. You will need a Claude plan and a few low-cost API keys, all explained in the setup.
